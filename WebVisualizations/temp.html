Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Lat). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year then the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of earth at the time of the year this data was gethered.
